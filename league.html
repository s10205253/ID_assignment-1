WHAT IS LEAGUE OF LEGENDS?

League of Legends is a team based strategy game where two teams of 5 people fight it out to destroy each others base. In this game you choose from a pool of over 140 champions which you control to kill enemy players and destroy the enemys base. To destroy the enemys base you will need to kill enemy players or kill the various minions and monsters that spawn around the map to get gold. You can then use the gold you aquired to buy items from the shop to deal more damage. On top of getting gold you will also need to get EXP by also killing enemy players and monsters.

WHY SHOULD YOU PLAY LEAGUE OF LEGENDS?

Amazing visuals

Firstly League of Legends has amazing graphics and visuals as seen in the video. The gameplay is smooth and visually appealing and there is no shortage of amazing cosmetics you can buy for your favourite champions called skins. Skins can change the visual effects of champions or add more visual effects to your champion which makes the game more enjoyable and fun.

Fierce and intense competitions

Secondly the competitions for league of legends are amazing. One notable example is the annual league of legends worlds championship, where the absolute best of the absolute best of the best teams from all over the world battle it out for the title of world champion and a prize pool of almost 2million dollars. The matches usually have alot of fast paced action in it and there are also talented casters that make the matches more engaging and fun.

Huge And Active Playerbase

Lastly League of legends has a huge playerbase with over 115 million people playing the game from all over the world, and for events like the worlds championship the live viewers peaked at 3.8 million viewers on twitch. Aside form official League events there are still alot of people watching individual League of Legends content creators and streamers like Tyler1 and Faker that average about 20 thousand viewers in their streams.
