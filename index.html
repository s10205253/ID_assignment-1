ABOUT US

Riot Games is a game company that was founded in 2006 with the goal of supporting player focused games. In 2009 they launched their first game called League of Legends which would go on to become one of the most played and popular pc game in the world. After the success of League of Legends they started to release other projects like music videos and multiple other games like Valorant and even Wild rift which is a mobile verison of League of Legends.
